## S (Problem Statement)

Mr. A was relieved when he saw that only poisonous mushrooms were around. Why?

## T (Truth)

Mr. A was lost in the mountains while mushroom picking. The fact that only poisonous mushrooms remained meant that someone had already picked the edible mushrooms—meaning people had been in the area. Realizing there were people nearby or other foragers close by, he was relieved knowing he had hope of being rescued.
